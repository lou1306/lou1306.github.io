I’m a PhD student in Computer Science at Gran Sasso Science Institute (GSSI).

My research focuses on specification and verification of agent-based models. Software systems are becoming pervasive in our everyday life, so you want them to be correct. I am trying to develop languages and tools that could help us with that.

Other stuff

I got my Master’s degree in Computer and Systems Engineering (Ingegneria Informatica e Automatica) at University of L’Aquila.

I am a Scout since 1998 and I’m a member of the Pattuglia Nazionale Informatica (National Patrol, Information Systems) pf my association.
